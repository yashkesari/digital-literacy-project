##  Project Overview

This repository is a **Digital Literacy Portfolio** created as part of the Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University. It documents practical skills across five major areas: visual communication, professional digital presence, coding platforms, email etiquette, and cybercrime awareness.

The portfolio demonstrates real-world digital competencies through hands-on tasks, screenshots, written reports, and supporting documents — all organized within this GitHub repository.

##  Task Summaries

### Task 1 – Infographic (Digital Literacy Presentation)
- **Tool Used:** Canva
- **Output:** An infographic covering digital literacy concepts including safe internet practices, essential student tools, and the importance of professional online presence.
- **File Location:** `task-1-presentation/`
- A PNG export of the infographic is included. The design focuses on clarity and visual appeal to communicate key digital literacy ideas effectively.

---

### Task 2 – Digital Profiles
- **Platforms Used:** GitHub, LinkedIn, Kaggle
- **Purpose:** To establish a professional digital identity across major platforms used in tech and academic communities.
- **File Location:** `task-2-portfolio/`
- Screenshots of all three profiles are included. The GitHub profile features a custom README with name, branch, year, and goals. LinkedIn includes education details from VIT Bhopal. Kaggle has a basic beginner profile set up for future data science work.

>  [View Portfolio Screenshots](https://github.com/yashkesari/digital-literacy-project/tree/main/task-2-portfolio)

---

###  Task 3 – Coding Platforms + Google Form
- **Platforms Used:** HackerRank, Google Forms, Google Sheets
- **Output:** Screenshot of solved HackerRank problem ("Solve Me First") + Digital Literacy Quiz form
- **File Location:** `task-3-platforms/`
- A Google Form titled **"Digital Literacy Quiz"** was created with 5 questions (MCQ and short answer). Responses are linked to a Google Sheet.

> 🔗 **Google Form Link:** [Digital Literacy Quiz](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd8zoIPTbPqJ2tt_dzHJci8TBSYNR0wJ2p0gJy7CCAEv1_5Pg/viewform?usp=publish-editor)


---

###  Task 4 – Email Etiquette + Social Media Checklist
- **Output:** Two professional emails + a social media do's and don'ts checklist
- **File Location:** `task-4-email-etiquette/`
- **Email 1:** Student writing to a professor requesting a deadline extension
- **Email 2:** Student writing to a company expressing internship interest
- A `social-media-checklist.txt` is also included with practical guidance on responsible digital behaviour.

---

###  Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness (Ransomware)
- **Topic:** Ransomware Attack – Case Study & Prevention
- **Output:** `casestudy.md` (200–250 words) + `prevention-checklist.txt`
- **File Location:** `task-5-cybercrime/`
- The case study explains what ransomware is, how it spreads, who it targets, and its consequences. The prevention checklist includes practical safety steps and references official resources.
